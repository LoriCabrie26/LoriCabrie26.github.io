About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Lindsay Cabrie and I'm 21 years old from Charlotte, NC. I got into acting while working in theme parks as an Atmosphere Character and have been looking for new ways to expand my abilities beyond that. I was recently casted in a university production of Measure for Measure, directed by Professor Rob Conkie, and had a wonderful experience there. Excited to be a part of this community!
